Legend PRM 

Legend was established in September 2017 by best friends and cofounders, Armani McKalpain and Treshawn Wray. Legend was created to inspire people to make a meaningful impact in their communities and on the world. A legend doesn’t have to claim the title. Based on the work they’ve done, their name will precede them.

Stay Ribelle

Stay Ribelle is a clothing brand created by Ryan Smith a current Junior at Michigan Sate University. The brand was created on October 29, 2019 and got its name from the ideal of staying true to you and being authentically 100% yourself. Stay Ribelle has a variety of T-shirts, pullovers, pants, and hats available on their website.
